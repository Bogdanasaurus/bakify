Elegantly intuitive, Bakify automatically formats anything you write and shows you analytical never words, really making your writing more positive. For example, the about page says Bakify is a “simple website” that inserts “MLA formatting” and scans for “analytical never-words” (Bakify 1). The narrator uses very good diction and imagery to show that Bakify helps you write better as a whole, and can fix anything you say. The monochromatic simplicity of Bakify’s design reflects the conciseness it employs on the text it is fixing. This proves that Bakify is an effective, elegant, and intuitive extension that instantaneously transforms your writing, elevating it to a higher level.








Please Note

  • This program will not magically fix your essay as it only finds analytical neverwords and offers synonymous terms.
  • Please put your text through a grammar checker and proofread after use.
